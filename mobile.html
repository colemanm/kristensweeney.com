About

Kristen was born and raised in sunny, beautiful Clearwater, Florida.  She fell in love with the stage at the age of 5, dancing to "Zip-a-dee-doo-dah" with a bluebird on her shoulder.  At 17, she was still singing and dancing around her living room, so she headed to the University of Florida to study musical theater.

After receiving her BFA, Kristen journeyed north to the Boston Conservatory for her Masters degree and her first real winter. Three years later, she was sufficiently frozen to make the move to NYC.

Performing has brought Kristen all over the U.S. and even offered international opportunities, and she is grateful for every step of this crazy, wonderful journey.  Kristen is also a Vinyasa yoga instructor, an avid reader, and an animal lover. Thanks for visiting!
